About Me

Living in South Park (no, not that one, the one in San Diego), I am a software engineer currently employed at Booz Allen Hamilton working on a big data platform and cyber awareness tool for the US Navy at the Space and Naval Warfare Systems Command in Point Loma, CA.

In addition to my amazing job, I am lucky enough to be a graduate student at Georgia Tech in the College of Computing. My specialization is Interactive Intelligence, which deals with human - computer interaction and artificial intelligence. I have a bachelor's degree in Managemenent Information Systems as well as Finance from Villanova University, and I also graduated from Dev Bootcamp.

I get to enjoy life with my wife Claire, a nurse in the Navy Nurse Corps, and our infant son Ronan, as well as our two Boston Terriers, Bailey and Cali. In my free time (is that still a thing?) I enjoy going on hikes, working out, playing guitar, boxing, and camping. Oh and of course the hobby I am best at, wasting time on YouTube.
